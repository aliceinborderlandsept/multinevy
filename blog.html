Navy Federal Credit Union – Benefits & Membership Guide

About Navy Federal

Navy Federal Credit Union (NFCU) is the largest credit union in the United States, serving over 13 million members including military personnel, veterans, and their families. Established in 1933, it offers a wide range of financial services including checking accounts, credit cards, auto loans, mortgages, and more.

Who Can Join?

Membership is open to:

  • Active duty and retired members of the Army, Marine Corps, Navy, Air Force, Coast Guard, and Space Force
  • Department of Defense civilians
  • Family members of existing NFCU members

Note: Navy Federal does not charge membership fees and offers 24/7 customer support.

Popular Products & Services

  • Zero-fee Checking & Savings accounts
  • Low-interest Credit Cards with rewards
  • VA Loans & Auto Financing
  • Mobile Banking & Bill Pay
